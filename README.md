# BUY ZONE CACULATOR

Caculates the best price range to buy stock base on historical behavior.

## What is the buy zone?

A buy zone in stocks refers to a price range or condition where a stock is considered a good buying opportunity based on various analytical factors, such as price momentum, valuation metrics, and historical trading behavior.

## How to use

1. Enter a stock tickect symbol in the search bar
2. Click caculate to determine the best zone.
